Biography

I am Sayeed. I graduated with Ph.D. in computer science from Florida International University in July, 2017. I had my Bachelors in Computer Science and Engineering from Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology in 2009. While being enrolled in my Ph.D. program, I also worked as a graduate assistant at FIU. Before that, I have worked as a software engineer at Enosis Solutions, and as a programmer at Flora Limited for total 3 years in my home country. There, I developed web and mobile applications, and banking solutions. My research interests are in data visualization, eye-tracking, and visual analytics. I have several publications in reputed journals. I am also proficient in several programming languages such as Java, C#, C/C++, Javascript, SQL, and Python. Moreover, I have expertise in Jquery, D3JS, AngularJS, ASP.net MVC, and Hadoop. In conclusion, I consider myself a hard working and passionate professional who loves doing his job.
